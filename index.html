Each emotion has 3 intensity levels:

  • Mild (Score: 1) - The weakest form, located at the outer edge of the wheel
  • Medium (Score: 2) - Moderate intensity, in the middle ring
  • Strong (Score: 3) - Most intense form, closest to the center

Dyad Score (0-6): When you combine two emotions, the total score is the sum of both intensities. For example:

  • Joy (Strong=3) + Trust (Strong=3) = 6/6 - Maximum Love
  • Joy (Medium=2) + Trust (Mild=1) = 3/6 - Gentle Affection
  • Joy (Mild=1) + Trust (Mild=1) = 2/6 - Subtle Connection

Choose emotions from the wheel or select a pre-defined dyad combination to explore different emotional blends!
